WINTER BREEZE

Winter breeze blew by

And all I could say was goodbye

Things that greeted back were

A satire and a cold heart.

It’s half past 10 and I’m still waiting for you

to come by.

Strangers, friends and all creatures walked past me

Strangers, friends, was what we were then

Love-ers – dead, is what we are now

no one knows what we will become, after

And fear elusions my soul.

Winter’s breeze - tickled nostrils

And all I did was sneeze

It will be a blessing, if you do come back

- Ato Donkor.
