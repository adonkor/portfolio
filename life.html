Life

As I live each day

Nothing about this life seems sensible

Today we laugh and love

Tomorrow we weep and hate

We seek that which is out of our reach

We acquire what we never thought of

Life, a paradox or an oxymoron

That I do not know.

For what I do know is the beauty it sheds in its

whimsical bliss.

A life unknown is beautiful.

A life known is history.

Everything that happens in this universe is fair.

We may not understand, but it is.

You know you have lived when you are ready for death.

So live a life that prepares you for death.

- Ato Donkor.
