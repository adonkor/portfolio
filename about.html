WELCOME MY FRIEND

Hello all my name is Ato Donkor. I am a Ghanaian American residing in Connecticut. I grew up in Ghana and then moved to America around age 15. My mom lived here before I moved here with my Dad and little brother I live with my brother and parent. My brother is younger than me. I use to live in a town called Dansoman in the Greater Accra region of Ghana. My Favorite foods are jollof a continental dish eaten in a few African countries like Nigeria, Liberia, Togo and Ghana, and beans and fried plantain. My extendended family are spread mainly in the Central, Ashanti and greater Accra regions of Ghana whiles my Nuclear families live mostly in Connecticut, Virginia and Massachussetts of the states.
